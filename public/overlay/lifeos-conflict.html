How It Works

When enabled, Lumin checks messages you write in the LifeOS chat for Gottman's Four Horsemen: contempt, criticism, defensiveness, and stonewalling. Detection is rule-based first (no AI cost). AI is only called on ambiguous cases.

When escalation is detected, Lumin offers a concrete rewrite suggestion — it never forces you to change what you wrote. Cancel window is always one action away.

Joint session monitoring requires separate consent from both parties under Settings → Conflict consent.
