A note before we begin

This space is here to support your healing work — not to replace professional support.

If you're working through something significant, consider doing this alongside a licensed therapist, grief counselor, or mental health professional. This system can go deep alongside that work.

If you're in crisis right now: call or text 988 (US Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) or your country's equivalent.
