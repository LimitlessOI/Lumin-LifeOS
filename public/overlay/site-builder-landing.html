How the lane should work

The goal is not to sell a giant site project first. The goal is to make the first yes easy, visible, and low risk.

1. Build the preview Scrape the current site, extract services and positioning, then generate a cleaner conversion-first homepage preview.
2. Show the contrast Short outreach: “We rebuilt your front door first. If you like it, we can make it yours.” The preview does most of the selling.
3. Climb the trust ladder Launch the site, then expand into GBP, SEO, reviews, intake, follow-up, receptionist, and full business operations.
