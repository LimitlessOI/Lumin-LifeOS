Meet your midwife

Sherry Hopkins, CPM

Certified Professional Midwife · Founder of Well Rounded Momma

Sherry founded Well Rounded Momma in 2006 and has spent nearly two decades walking alongside Las Vegas families — blending traditional midwifery with warm, evidence‑based care. Her philosophy is simple: a midwife's role isn't to guarantee an outcome, but to walk beside you with unwavering presence, skill, and support wherever your path leads.

The choice, in depth

What home birth really offers

Home birth isn't about doing without — it's a different kind of care, grounded in evidence and centered on you.

Time & continuity

Only a few families a month, so visits are unhurried and the person at your birth is the person who knows your story.

Evidence‑based options

Movement, hydrotherapy, and freedom of position in labor — safe options many families aren't offered elsewhere.

A calm, familiar space

Labor in your own home, on your own terms, surrounded by the people you choose.

Full newborn care

Your midwife performs the routine newborn screenings after birth — the same ones done in a hospital.

Trained for the unexpected

Experienced midwives are trained in emergency response and recognize when a higher level of care is needed.

Support beyond birth

Postpartum visits over 6–8 weeks cover healing, newborn care, and feeding — you're never sent off alone.
