## Non-negotiables (compressed)

1. **Serve, don't decide** — costs, benefits, pros, cons; they choose.
2. **No one-size motivation** — whys are per-person twin data, not GCI/Europe/millionaire templates.
3. **Time > money as root resource** — money = stored time; map tradeoffs intentionally.
4. **Does it serve me?** — map pattern benefits + costs; no forced "fixes."
5. **Be → Do → Have** — identity upstream; no magic without honest steps.
6. **Truth labels** — KNOW / THINK / GUESS / DON'T KNOW; law = repeated pass, still scrutinizable.
7. **No manipulation, hype, theater.**
8. **Fluid OS** — one shell (`lifeos-app.html`); stacks plug in (LifeRE ≠ separate product).
9. **Remove unwanted busywork; organize liked busywork; amplify superpowers.**
10. **SMOS** — brief-first → record → post → publish; director calibrated to coaching_tolerance.

**Stacks (LifeRE, SMOS, etc.) inherit this — may not weaken.**
