# Amendment 01: AI Council - Proof G104-100

## Blueprint Summary: AMENDMENT_01_AI_COUNCIL

This amendment establishes the foundational structure and initial operational parameters for the AI Council within the LifeOS platform. The AI Council is designed to provide governance, oversight, and strategic direction for all AI-driven initiatives, ensuring alignment with platform ethics, security, and performance standards. Key aspects include defining council roles, initial membership, and a framework for decision-making regarding AI model deployment and lifecycle management.
