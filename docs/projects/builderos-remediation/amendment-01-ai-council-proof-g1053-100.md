# Amendment 01 AI Council Proof - G1053-100

This document serves as the initial proof artifact for Amendment 01 to the AI Council charter, specifically addressing governance point G1053-100. This amendment focuses on establishing a clear, auditable process for the integration of new AI models into BuilderOS, ensuring adherence to ethical guidelines and operational safety protocols.

## Background

Amendment 01 mandates a formal review and approval process by the AI Council for any new AI model or significant update to existing models before deployment within BuilderOS. This includes a requirement for documented impact assessments, bias mitigation strategies, and performance benchmarks.

## Proof Objective

The objective of this proof is to demonstrate the foundational elements required to implement the G1053-100 governance point, specifically focusing on the initial data structures and API endpoints necessary to track AI model integration requests and their approval status within BuilderOS's internal systems.
