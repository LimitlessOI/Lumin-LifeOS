# Amendment 14: White-Label Proof - G24-100 - Dynamic Logo Integration

## Proof Point Summary
This document details the successful proof of concept for dynamically loading and displaying a tenant-specific logo within the LifeOS platform, as part of Amendment 14's white-labeling initiative.

## Objective
To demonstrate the capability of the LifeOS platform to retrieve and render a unique brand logo based on the authenticated tenant's configuration, without requiring a redeploy.

## Scope
-   Integration with an existing tenant configuration service to fetch `tenant.branding.logoUrl`.
