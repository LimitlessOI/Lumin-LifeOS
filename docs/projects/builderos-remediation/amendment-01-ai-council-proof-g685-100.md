# Amendment 01 AI Council Proof: G685-100 - BuilderOS Remediation

## 1. Introduction
This document details the proof-of-concept and remediation plan for integrating Amendment 01 AI Council directives into the BuilderOS platform. The primary goal is to establish automated policy enforcement for AI-related artifacts within the build pipeline, ensuring compliance with the AI Council's governance mandates. This remediation addresses the need for robust validation and control over AI model deployment and usage within the LifeOS ecosystem.
