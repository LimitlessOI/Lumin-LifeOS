# Amendment 01: AI Council Proof - G35-100 Blueprint Note

This document outlines the next smallest build slice to operationalize the AI Council's oversight within BuilderOS, specifically addressing the proof point G35-100.

The current BuilderOS lacks a formal, auditable mechanism to record and verify AI Council approvals for specific BuilderOS projects or actions, as mandated by Amendment 01. Proof G35-100 requires the ability to record that a specific BuilderOS project has received AI Council approval
