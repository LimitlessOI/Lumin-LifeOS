## 1. The One Engine

There is not a Website Engine, a Video Engine, a Marketing Engine, and a Life Engine. There is **one engine** with different **renderers** on top (website, video, email, ad, social post, sales script, coaching conversation, AI-receptionist call).

The engine answers one question:

> **Given what we know about this person right now, what is the next *voluntary* step that creates the greatest long-term value for them?**

- **External name (customer-facing):** *Creative Engine* — simple, unremarkable, not exposed publicly. **Locked, not open to debate.**
- **Internal name:** does not matter and is not public. The functions inside it: mission contract, journey positioning, earned-attention budget, objection modeling, competitive-gap analysis, confidence scoring, decision simulation, outcome learning.

The customer buys creative output. Internally, the system produces it by optimizing the next **truthful, voluntary** step — not by generating content.

## 2. The Laws (candidate)

Each law is tagged with the **Law-Category Test** (see §4) so we never inflate a strategy into a law.

**Law 1 — Reality is the scoreboard.** *(empirical)* Not confidence, not opinion, not intuition. Every prediction, taste-score, forecast, or persona-critique is a **hypothesis** until reality measures it. Founder wording (preferred, because lived): *"Results — often harsh, always fair. Measure them. If you don't like the results, make different choices."* Short handle: "reality is the scoreboard."

**Law 2 — People own their goals.** *(governance)* The system never decides what someone *should* want; it helps them get what *they* decided matters. If they change their mind, that is success, not failure.

**Law 3 — Always earn the next step.** *(governance/strategy)* Never ask for trust, attention, commitment, or a sale you have not earned. Content is a chain of micro-contracts (earn 3s → 10s → 30s → …). Optimize for the next *willing* step, not the final outcome.

**Law 4 — Influence without coercion.** *(governance; falsifiable proxy)* Never remove a person's ability to freely choose. **Falsifiable proxy (the actual gate):** did the system re-pursue the original ask after an explicit opt-out? **Format-level rule:** no growth incentive (referral pressure, upsell, streak-shame) may override an opt-out. *The real test of "no manipulation" is what happens when someone says no.*

**Law 5 — Build capability, not dependency.** *(governance)* If someone needs the system forever, the system failed. Purpose is increasing agency until they need it less. **OPEN:** the business-model tension (subscription incentives reward retention) is unresolved — lead candidate is Willhite's "paid in proportion to service rendered": a graduated user refers/expands across the ecosystem rather than churns.

**Law 6 — Progress happens in relationship. *(proposed addition)*** *(governance)* The system supports the person's connection to **real people** and never substitutes itself for it. Rationale: Self-Determination Theory's third validated need (relatedness) is otherwise unaddressed; and a system that becomes a person's *only* relationship is building dependency — so Law 6 also **guards Law 5**.

**Companion principle (pairs with Law 1):** *Reality tells us **whether** we succeeded; human dignity constrains **how** we are allowed to succeed.* A manipulative message that gets more clicks is not adopted just because reality says it "worked." Law 1 and Laws 2/4 are coupled on purpose.

## 3. Enforcement architecture — hard-gate the reasoning, not the behavior

Nothing renders until a structured intake exists: **Mission · Customer · current/desired decision stage · primary objection · evidence required · trust strategy · next voluntary action · success metric.** The exact fields are not sacred; forcing the questions to be answered is.

- **Already a proven pattern here:** BPB requires a spec before Architect/Builder acts; SENTRY requires assertions before a step is "done." Same shape, new domain.
- **Necessary but not sufficient — the coherence layer:** a presence-check can be gamed by a placeholder (the "13 bugs" lesson: an exports-smoke check passed garbage because it only checked a name existed). The gate needs a second, **adversarial coherence check** — does the Objection follow from the Customer stage; does the Trust Strategy address the stated Objection — the same "verify-the-factory" principle as the SENTRY chaos-canary, aimed at content.
- **Decision Brief / simulate-before-commit** (= Stoic *premeditatio malorum*): for **significant** goals, simulate costs/benefits/risks/uncertainties before commit, then compare to reality after N months (calibration loop). **Stakes threshold required** (Gate Charter: block only irreversible/high-blast-radius) or it becomes analysis-paralysis and violates Law 3. Career/financial/major-health = full brief; a daily micro-step = no brief, just forward motion.
- **Decision Simulator** (persona critique before publish) is **Hypothesis-grade** until its scores are calibrated against real outcomes (Law 1 applied to itself) — route through the existing calibration-sampling mechanism or it is theater in a UX costume.

## 4. The Law-Category Test (governance tool)

Before anything is called a **Law**, classify it:

1. **Empirical** — a testable claim about reality (e.g. Law 1). Can be a Law.
2. **Governance** — a procedurally-verifiable rule (e.g. "the AI never overrode the stated goal" — checkable yes/no). Can be a Law.
3. **Default strategy** — itself *subject to Law 1* (e.g. Be→Do→Have, coaching style, media routing). **Never a Law** — it is the current best-evidence default, replaced when reality shows better for a specific person.

This prevents philosophy-inflation and keeps the doctrine falsifiable rather than dogmatic.

## 5. One substrate, two graphs

The same **predict → measure → calibrate** primitive already shipped (Cognitive Core Outcome Oracle + self-repair Tier-0 stores) underlies code repair, marketing decisions, *and* life-coaching. Build **one substrate, two instantiations** — not parallel systems:

- **Constitutional Knowledge Graph** (company level): principles as nodes, domains as edges; queried by "which principles apply to this new problem," not by chat history. Partial precursors already exist (`self-repair-root-cause-chains.js`, the Unified Doctrine Map) — both flat tables today, not yet a graph.
- **Life Graph** (per user): Identity as the center node; Health/Business/Relationships/Purpose/Learning as edges that ripple into each other. Cold-start from best general human-development evidence, personalize from real interaction (same cold-start pattern as a fresh codegen target's reputation score).

**Guardrail (protects Law 2):** if the system models "the kind of person who builds wealth" it must offer those as **hypotheses for the person to evaluate**, never prescribe them — otherwise the system chooses the goal by the back door.
