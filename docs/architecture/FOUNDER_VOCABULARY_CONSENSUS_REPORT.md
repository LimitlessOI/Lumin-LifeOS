## Part G — Products · REPs · not departments

**Products:** LifeOS, LimitlessOS, MarketingOS, BuilderOS (platform), etc.

**REPs:** Context capsules — LifeOS REP, Relationship REP, Founder REP, Revenue REP, …  
Not seats. Not AIs. Not votes. Catalog grows with one-line AM48 receipt.

**Authority vs REP capsules (separate stacks):**
- **Authority** — dept law (SNT rules, CDR zero-decision contract, BPB translate-only)

Load order: authorities → REPs → session evidence.
