## Founder lock

To scale, **business owners do production through our app**. We direct them. We show **true cost** at each layer. We help — not hand them a black-box agency bill.

**SocialMediaOS is the producer and director:**

1. Interview the business and owner (coach session).  
2. Direct shots, lighting, sound — redo until pass.  
3. If they pay an external production company instead → fine, but cost is higher and we show that honestly.  
4. **Generic tier** vs **real-time / changing video tier** — different costs, both visible upfront.  
5. First videos from BusinessOS onboarding **auto-publish to connected socials** (with consent).  
6. Upsell bundles: *"For $X/month we also build your website, automate ops, …"* — progressive price points.

---

## Cost representation rules

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **True cost** | Show vendor quote + our orchestration fee + AI compute where applicable |
| **Compare honestly** | More expensive vendor with great reviews → sell on why. Expensive + weak reviews → exclude or warn |
| **DIY vs done-for-you** | App-guided shoot (low) → SMOS polish (mid) → external agency (high) |
| **No hidden layers** | Real-time generative video/ad variants = explicit line item |
