## Print pricing research (2026 — get live quote before buy)

Approximate US pricing for **3″ × 3″ die-cut vinyl with laminate** (sources: Sticker Mule, VistaPrint, industry comparators). **Verify on vendor site before purchase.**

| Qty | Sticker Mule (approx) | Budget / VistaPrint (approx) | Per-sticker band (market) |
|----:|----------------------:|-----------------------------:|--------------------------:|
| 50 | ~$60 ($1.20/ea) | ~$45–$70 | $0.90–$1.50 |
| 100 | ~$73 ($0.73/ea) | ~$70 | $0.70–$1.10 |
| 500 | ~$152 ($0.30/ea) | ~$180 | $0.35–$0.55 |
| 1,000 | ~$232 ($0.23/ea) | ~$180–450 | $0.23–$0.45 |
| 5,000 | ~$723 ($0.15/ea) | lower per-unit | $0.15–$0.25 |

**Notes:**

- **Sticker Mule** — premium vinyl, free shipping, free proofs, ~4-day turnaround; best for quality-sensitive brand kits.  
- **VistaPrint** — often wins on **bulk budget**; thinner vinyl; good for mass handouts.  
- **Factory-direct** (e.g. Stickerine-style) — often $300–450 / 1k range; compare quotes.  
- Holographic / metallic finishes: **+30–60%**. Rush: **+$10–25** or percentage.

**Recommendation for first run:** order **50–100** proof run from Sticker Mule (quality check), then **500–1k** from best quote if used in onboarding kits.
