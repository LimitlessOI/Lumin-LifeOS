## What this means for LifeOS specifically

LifeOS is one **renderer** on the same engine that powers the Creative Engine (marketing) — the coaching conversation is the renderer; the engine underneath asks *"what is the next voluntary step this person is both capable of and willing to take toward the goal they own?"*

**Coaching principles (LifeOS-facing):**
- **Hold capable, not accountable.** "I believe you're capable — what made this difficult?" not streak-shame.
- **Silence is data.** Log *why* a prompt didn't land (too personal / wrong timing / too abstract / too much effort); try a different mode (question/story/humor/data/challenge/reflection). Learn how *this* person engages. **Routing note:** this coaching channel must respect **SO-003** — never a cheap-tier/canned response at a moment that matters.
- **Three scores per interaction:** Progress · Engagement · Emotional Cost (progress that raises tomorrow's resistance is bad coaching).
- **Constitutional council review** before an interaction: Coach / Psychologist / Teacher / Behavior Scientist / Empathy Advocate / Chair.

**The Laws, as they bind LifeOS:**
1. Reality is the scoreboard ("results, harsh but fair — measure, then change the choice").
2. People own their goals (change-of-mind is success).
3. Earn the next willing step (never the final outcome).
4. Influence without coercion — **honor the "no"** (system may not re-pursue after an explicit opt-out; no incentive overrides it).
5. Build capability, not dependency (if they need LifeOS forever, LifeOS failed).
6. **(proposed)** Progress happens in relationship — LifeOS strengthens the person's connection to real people, never replaces it.

**Development model:** **Be → Do → Have**, understood as a loop with **Have(vivid vision) → Become → Do → Have(realized)** (Darren Hardy's *Compound Effect* example). Treated as a **Category-3 default strategy** — subject to Law 1, personalized per person, not dogma. Identity is the center node.

**Life Graph:** Health / Business / Relationships / Purpose / Learning ripple into each other and into Identity. **Limitless (business) + LifeOS (personal) are integrated** because a life is integrated — only the bank accounts stay un-comingled. Cold-start from best general human-development evidence, personalize from reality.

**Guardrail (protects Law 2):** any "kind of person who…" identity model is offered as a **hypothesis for the person to evaluate**, never prescribed.
